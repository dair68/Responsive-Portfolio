About Me

I am an aspiring programmer currently studying applied math at UCLA. Topics I enjoy include computation, differential equations, and mathematical modeling. I love solving puzzles and taking on new challenges. I've made programs in C++, Java, and Python, and aim to add more languages to the list!

In my free time I like to play videogames and the piano. My favorite videogames include Super Metroid , Shin Megami Tensei IV, and Nine Persons, Nine Hours, Nine Doors. My favorite songs to play on the piano are "Lost in Thoughts All Alone" from Fire Emblem Fates and "Bluebird Lamentation" from Virtue's Last Reward. Nothing is more invigorating than slowly taming and mastering a formerly daunting piece.

Every day I'm always learning, always sharpening the mind. One day I hope to combine all my interests and create a simple game of my own, with original musical compositions and puzzles anyone can enjoy.
